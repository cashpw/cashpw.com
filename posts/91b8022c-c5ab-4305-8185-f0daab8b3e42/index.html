How to become an expert

  • Dec 26, 2023
  • Jan 10, 2026
  • 1 min

You become an expert by building up explicit and tacit knowledge; because expertise is pattern matching based on known Frames.

Traditionally an expert is made through study (for the explicit) and experience (for the tacit). They do the relevant thing for a long time and thereby gaining a library they can draw on, consciously and unconsciously, to make decisions. However, we can speed up the process by working smart.

What’s the most effective way to acquire explicit and tacit knowledge? The answer depends on the subject matter:

  • Use Deliberate practice for subjects with a “long history of well-established pedagogy” (Chin 2020) (for example: math, music, and chess)
  • Use Purposeful practice for other subjects (for example: business, sales, sport, art, singing, writing, poetry, etc).
