Be explicit

  • Feb 20, 2022
  • Jan 4, 2026
  • 1 min

Prefer explicit, rather than implicit, writing. Explicit writing:

  • minimizes misinterpretation
  • reduces cognitive load on the reader
  • is easier for newcomers, or those without context Newcomers (or yourself in the future)
  • encourages correctness and easy verification
