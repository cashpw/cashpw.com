The Dreamtime is a finite period of time in which people have enough Slack and Excess resources to resist Moloch/Collective action problem/Red Queen’s race. This is the time of Non-zero-sum games. It’s a time where dreams are real.

An example of dreamtime would be the time between the discovery of new arable land and the saturation of that land.

Dreamtime starts when something new happens or is discovered which changes the quantity or efficiency of stuff:

  • Technological advances (e.g. fertilizers, genetically modified crops, factories, automation, and engines) which increase our production superlinearly (i.e. We can produce 2x more with <2x human labor)
  • Discovery of resources which were either unknown or inaccessible (e.g. new continents)
  • Relative increase in resources through a decrease in population (e.g. the Black Death)

Yet even these differences [between modern humans and our decendents] will pale relative to one huge difference: our lives are far more dominated by consequential delusions: wildly false beliefs and non-adaptive values that matter. While our descendants may explore delusion-dominated virtual realities, they will well understand that such things cannot be real, and don’t much influence history. In contrast, we live in the brief but important “dreamtime” when delusions drove history. Our descendants will remember our era as the one where the human capacity to sincerely believe crazy non-adaptive things, and act on those beliefs, was dialed to the max"
