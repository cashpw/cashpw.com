Write to remember

  • Nov 20, 2025
  • Jan 12, 2026
  • 1 min
Table of Contents
  • Expand

Write things down, and make them discoverable, so you can refer back to them later and refresh your memory. It’s not about remembering everything you ever encounter. As an example: I like to go back through my notes on books I’ve read (reading quotes and reflections) as the topic re-emerges in my life, or just for fun.

Additionally, the act of writing them down helps embed them in your memory even if you never come back to your writing.
