Structured writing uses chapters/sections, headings, lists, call-out quotes, tables, and more, to organize and present its content.

Advantages of structured writing #

  • Provides multiple entry and exit points
  • Helps readers understand the flow of topics
  • Helps you organize your own thoughts
  • Allows for easier skimming or retrieval
  • Limits local complexity by dividing it up into chunks
  • Avoids a ‘wall of text’ which may dissuade readers

Disadvantages of structured writing #

  • less dense than unstructured writing; requires more (white)space
