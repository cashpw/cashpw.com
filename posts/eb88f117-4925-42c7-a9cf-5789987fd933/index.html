Evergreen notes

  • Feb 6, 2022
  • Dec 24, 2025

Andy Matuschak’s idea of an evergreen note is one which is “written and organized to evolve, contribute, and accumulate over time, across projects”. I see it as the Unix philosophy applied to writing and note taking.
