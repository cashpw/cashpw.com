About #

This is my personal website, notes, digital garden, blog, zettelkasten, and so forth. Writing is learning and thinking, sharing, memory, and it scales. I write about thoughts I have, what I read, my career and its culture, photography, ceramics, parenting, and more. My writing is densely linked, evergreen, and useful.

I’m employed as a software engineer and live near San Jose, California, USA.
