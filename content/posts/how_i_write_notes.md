The details of how I write and publish the notes in my [personal knowledge management]({{< relref "personal_knowledge_management.md" >}}) system.

-   Writing: I write and publish using Emacs -- specifically Doom Emacs ([my config](https://github.com/cashpw/dotfiles/blob/main/config/doom/config.org)) -- org-mode and a combination of org-roam and ox-hugo.
-   Public-facing notes: [Github](https://github.com/cashpw/cashpw.com)
-   Citations: Citar and Zotra
-   Files (PDF, images, etc): Emacs using [attachments](https://orgmode.org/manual/Attachments.html) and org-noter; [org-download](https://github.com/abo-abo/org-download)
-   [Style Guide]({{< relref "writing_style_guide.md" >}})
